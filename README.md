# MaRisk Internal Audit Playbook

This repository provides a practical internal audit playbook
for assessing compliance with the German Minimum Requirements
for Risk Management (MaRisk), focusing on key areas:

- Governance (AT 4.3.1)
- IT Risk Management (AT 7.2)
- Business Continuity Management (AT 7.3)
- Outsourcing & Third-Party Risk (AT 9)

## Purpose
- Demonstrate knowledge of central bank regulatory framework
- Provide structured audit modules for internal review
- Prepare for supervisory inspections

## Disclaimer
This repository is a mock-up for educational and demonstration purposes.
